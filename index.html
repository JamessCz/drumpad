Drumpad

Created by: Jakub Horáček

How to play?

  1. Press 1 to play Closed Hi-Hat.
  2. Press 2 to play Snare.
  3. Press 3 to play Kick.
  4. Press 4 to play Tom 1.
  5. Press 5 to play Tom 2.
  6. Press 6 to play Floor Tom.
  7. Press 7 to play Crash.
  8. Press 8 to play Splash.
  9. Press 9 to play Ride.

Settings

  1. Hi-Hat Toggle
  2. Snare/Rim Toggle
  3. ▼Download the app
